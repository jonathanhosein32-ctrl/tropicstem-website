Meet Our Founder 💐

The vision behind TropicStem Florals

Our Story Begins Here

TropicStem Florals was founded by a passionate young entrepreneur, JONATHAN HOSEIN, with a vision to transform the floral industry in Trinidad and Tobago. Inspired by the natural beauty of the Caribbean and the emotional power of flowers, the founder sought to create a brand that blends creativity, culture, and convenience.

Recognizing the lack of modern, accessible floral services, the founder introduced an innovative approach—combining traditional floral artistry with a seamless online experience. This vision has allowed TropicStem Florals to stand out as a brand that not only delivers flowers but creates meaningful experiences for every customer.

Driven by a commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, the founder continues to push boundaries, ensuring that every arrangement reflects elegance, freshness, and authenticity.

“Flowers are not just gifts — they are stories waiting to be told.”

– Founder, TropicStem Florals
